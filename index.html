balloons love care

Looking after your balloons

Room Temperature

Keep balloons indoors and away from heat sources to prevent expansion.

Avoid Direct Sun

Keep away from direct sunlight or heat to stop balloons from popping.

Avoid Cold Spots

Don't leave in cold spaces like garages — balloons may deflate in the cold.

Handle With Care

Keep away from sharp objects, popcorn ceilings, pets and small children.
